ABOUT THE SHOW

Where Tech, Politics, and Absurdity Collide.

The search is over. You've been looking for us your entire life, and we've been waiting. Welcome to While We're On The Subject ("WWOTS" for short), the show that features interviews with notorious entrepreneurs, haughty politicians...and even a few above average impersonations, in just 15 minutes! Hosted by two millennial technologists, one brilliant, the other... loud, WWOTS is short, sweet and fun doses of the stuff of substance.

RYAN Bethea, CO-HOST

Ryan is an influence marketing consultant at the intersection of politics, tech and Hollywood. Ryan led grassroots marketing for the #6 most successful political documentary of all time, AMERICA (Lionsgate), and now heads up grassroots marketing for PragerU, the fastest growing conservative non-profit online.

AARON GINN, CO-HOST

Aaron is an experienced Silicon Valley technologist and co-founder of Lincoln. He is one of the original thought leaders of the growth hacking and conservatarian movements. He worked on growth for Everlane, StumbleUpon, and Mitt Romney's 2012 campaign.
